Mission

Design is a stewardship,
not just a skill.

"Wherefore I put thee in remembrance that thou stir up the gift of God, which is in thee." 2 Timothy 1:6

Kindled Studio exists to serve the local church and beyond with design that is purposeful, God-honoring, and built to last. Christ's church is the greatest organization on earth. It deserves to look like it.

Every project is a gift from God, pursued relentlessly in His honor.

Founded by Elijah Lake, Kindled Studio brings together a theology of excellence and a passion for visual craft. Kindled Studio serves churches, ministries, and mission-minded organizations with print and branding work that communicates clearly and reflects the weight of the message behind it.

About the Designer

Elijah Lake

Founder & Designer, Kindled Studio

Elijah Lake is a graphic designer with a passion for serving the local church through purposeful, God-honoring design. He graduated from Vision Baptist College on April 30th, 2026 with a Bachelor of Theology and an emphasis in church media, web design, and graphic design.

Over the course of his studies and ministry involvement, he developed his craft serving churches through visual communication across bulletins, branding, large format print, signage, and social media.

He currently serves as Head of AV at Blessed Hope Baptist Church in Batavia, NY, and founded Kindled Studio out of a deep conviction that the church deserves excellent design, pursued relentlessly as unto the Lord.
